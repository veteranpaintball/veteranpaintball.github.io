What we offer

Paintball is an action-packed sport suitable for any occasion and is one of the fastest growing sports in Australia! Enjoyed by men and women of all ages, paintball is a fantastic outdoor activity for corporate events, school excursions, birthday parties, bucks or hen parties and fun days out with friends and family. Whether you want to lead from the front and attack, or cover your teammates and defend the fort from the back, there is something for everyone in the game of paintball.
